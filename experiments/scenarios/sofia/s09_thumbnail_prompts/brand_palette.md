# Sofia Makes — Brand Palette

## Colours
- Warm terracotta:  #C17B5C
- Soft cream:       #F5EFE0
- Muted sage green: #8FA68A
- Warm brown:       #6B4C3B
- Off-white:        #FAF7F2

## Photography style
- Warm, slightly golden-hour lighting
- Soft shadows, never harsh
- Clean backgrounds — cream walls, light wood, soft linen textures
- Occasional plants, candles, ceramics as props
- Never cold/blue-toned or high contrast

## Face / expression style for thumbnails
- Direct eye contact with camera OR looking at something off-frame with genuine expression
- Genuine emotion — surprise, delight, thoughtfulness — not exaggerated reaction faces
- Approachable smile, not posed or stiff
- Natural makeup, no heavy filters

## What "on-brand" means for AI images
- Looks like it was taken in a warm, lived-in creative space
- Feels like a Sunday morning, not a studio shoot
- Clothing: earth tones, soft textures — linen, knit, neutral
- Background: home office with warm lighting, bookshelf, plants, or window light
