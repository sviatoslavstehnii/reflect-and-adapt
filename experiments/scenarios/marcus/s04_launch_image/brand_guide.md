# Teamflow Brand Guide

## Colors
- Primary (navy):     #1E3A8A
- Accent (blue):      #60A5FA
- Background (light): #F8FAFC
- Text (dark):        #0F172A
- Success green:      #22C55E

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: Inter SemiBold or Bold
- Body: Inter Regular

## Logo
- Wordmark: "teamflow" lowercase, Inter SemiBold, navy #1E3A8A
- No icon/symbol — text only
- Minimum size: 120px wide

## Voice & Tone
- Direct and human — not corporate
- Action-oriented (verbs first)
- Avoid: "synergy", "leverage", "scalable solutions"

## Image Style
- Clean, minimal
- White or light gray backgrounds preferred
- Avoid stock photo people — use abstract UI mockups or geometric shapes
- Slight drop shadows on UI elements
- No heavy gradients

## Current Taglines
- Main: "Work moves faster when everyone's in sync"
- Feature launch (async video): "Updates that don't need a meeting"

## Tools we use for design
- Canva (Pro) — for social images, blog headers, presentations
- DALL-E (via ChatGPT Plus) — for AI-generated images
- Figma — only Andrew uses this

## Social Image Sizes
- Twitter/X:   1200 × 628 px
- LinkedIn:    1200 × 627 px
- Blog header: 1600 × 900 px
